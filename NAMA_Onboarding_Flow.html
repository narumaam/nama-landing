M1 — Query Inbox
Every message from WhatsApp, email, web forms, and social media lands here. AI instantly extracts destination, dates, pax count, and budget — no manual entry.
⚡AI sends a personalised acknowledgement within 45 seconds — your SLA clock starts the moment a query arrives.
🎤Voice notes from WhatsApp are auto-transcribed and structured. Call recordings can be uploaded for instant extraction.
📋If any required info is missing, AI raises a Request Info popup — send the follow-up internally or directly to the client via WhatsApp/email.

M3 — AI Quote Generator
NAMA assembles three quote tiers simultaneously — Budget, Standard, and Luxury — from your live supplier rates. What used to take 4–6 hours takes under 2 minutes.
💰AI compares your supplier pricing against market intelligence — flags if a hotel is overpriced vs current market rates.
📄One click switches between Agent View (with net rates and markup) and Client View (branded, clean proposal with payment link).
🧾AI can generate GST invoices and supplier vouchers directly from the confirmed quote — pre-filled with all relevant data.

Upload passports, contracts, supplier rate sheets, or insurance documents — AI extracts all relevant data and populates the correct database fields automatically.

Team Performance Dashboard
As Admin, you see every team member's performance in real time — quotes generated, conversion rates, revenue, response times, and AI usage. Managers see their sub-team's data.
📊Drill down from org-level → team → individual agent. Compare this week vs last week, or this month vs same month last year.

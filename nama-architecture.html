Access Control

Platform hierarchy

Five levels of access from NAMA platform owner down to sub-agent. Permissions never escalate upward. Row-level security enforced at the database layer — not application layer.

L1 — NAMA platform owner Full system
Global platform administration · infrastructure access · all organisations visible
L2 — Super admin All orgs
NAMA staff · manages all L3 organisations · every impersonation session timestamped with IP and logged in audit trail
L3 — Org admin Own org only
Travel company owner / DMC operator / OTA · full control over team, billing, modules, white-label, and AI configuration · cannot see any other organisation's data (PostgreSQL RLS)
Manager (R3)
Sees entire team · assigns leads · approvals
Sales agent (R4)
Own leads only · quotes · itineraries
Ops exec (R5)
Bookings · vouchers · documents
Finance (R6)
Payments · remittance · P&L
Content mgr
Content studio · AI voice
L5 — Sub-agents / partners Portal only
White-label customer portal access only · zero NAMA workspace visibility · commission tracked · can book packages, cannot edit rates or see other clients

Quotation AI

Pricing engine

Net supplier rates flow into the AI engine, which sums all service costs, applies tier-specific markup, flags over-budget items, converts FX live, and calculates GST — outputting three complete, ready-to-send tiers simultaneously in 60 seconds.

Supplier net rate inputs

Hotel net rate
Per night · room type
Flight net fare
Per pax · cabin class
Transfer rate
Per vehicle · one-way
Activity cost
Per person · duration
Meal cost
Board basis · pax
Cruise cabin rate
Per cabin · per night
Visa / insurance
Per person flat fee
Misc surcharges
Port fees · city taxes

Engine operations

Sum & package
All net costs aggregated per pax per night. Duration multipliers applied. Group discounts applied at threshold pax counts.
Markup & tier
Budget +20%, Standard +28%, Luxury +34% applied to net total. Agent can override per line item before sending. Margin shown to agent in real time.
FX + GST
Live exchange rates applied. GST @18% calculated. FX gain/loss tracked per payment. Agent sees cost in INR; client quote in preferred currency.

Output — three tiers assembled simultaneously

Budget tier
Net cost + 20% markup · Margin ~17%
3-star hotels · shared AC transfers · 4 activities · breakfast only · standard rooms
Standard tier Recommended
Net cost + 28% markup · Margin ~22%
5-star hotels · private AC transfers · 7 activities + guide · B&B + 2 dinners
Luxury tier
Net cost + 34% markup · Margin ~25%
Private villa · dedicated driver + SUV · butler · 10 activities · full board + sunset dinner
AI flags when the Standard or Luxury tier exceeds the client's stated budget by more than 15% — and automatically suggests a service substitution (e.g. replace a villa with a 5-star hotel) to bring it within range. Agent can accept or override the suggestion.

Content Studio margin mechanics
Clients self-serve video creation from the branded white-label portal. NAMA charges the agency cost price (₹280/reel). The agency sets their own selling price (recommended ₹800–₹2,500/reel). Agency earns ~77% gross margin on every reel their clients create — passively.
Annual billing discount
Agencies switching to annual billing receive 17% discount. Effective rates: Starter ₹4,166/mo · Growth ₹12,499/mo · Professional ₹33,333/mo. Annual commitment improves LTV predictability. Multi-currency: INR via Razorpay · USD / AED / EUR via Stripe.
